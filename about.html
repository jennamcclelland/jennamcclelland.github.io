I say that one must be a seer, make oneself a seer. The poet becomes a seer by a long, prodigious, and rational disordering of all the senses.

- Arthur Rimbaud, Letter to Paul Demeny, 1871

I am a visual artist, writer, musician, baker, and curator with an honours degree from the University of Toronto in English Literature, German Language & Cultural Studies and Visual Arts, and I have a particular interest in the grotesque and the female body.

I am primarily based in Toronto, although I have nomadic tendencies.
