I say that one must be a seer, make oneself a seer. The poet becomes a seer by a long, prodigious, and rational disordering of all the senses.

- Arthur Rimbaud, Letter to Paul Demeny, 1871

Jenna McClelland is a visual artist, writer, musician, and curator with an honours degree from the University of Toronto in English Literature, German Language & Cultural Studies and Visual Arts.

Jenna is particularly interested in the grotesque and the female body.

Jenna is based in Toronto.

Jenna always aims to dislodge her senses with the goal of “sight” (she’s still figuring out what that means).

This is her resume.
